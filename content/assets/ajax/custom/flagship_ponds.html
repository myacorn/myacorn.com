Flagship Ponds in Wales
for Pond Conservation

myAcorn have developed a web-based monitoring form and database for UK charity Pond Conservation. The form enables surveyors to assess possible threats to 'Flagship Pond' sites. The form records basic water chemistry and plant information and uses this to make an automatic calculation of risk. Anything that triggers a moderate or high risk will generate an email to the relevant parties responsible for looking after that pond.
