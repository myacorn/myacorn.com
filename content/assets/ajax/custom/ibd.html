Predicting Serious Drug Side Effects
in Gastroenterology
for the UK IBD Genetics Consortium

myAcorn have developed a new website for a study being conducted by the UK IBD Genetics Consortium. The aim of the study is to predict drug side effects in Inflammatory Bowel Disease and the site includes seven case report forms linked to an online database. Staff at participating hospitals are able to enter patient data into the secure online database via the web forms. The data can then be downloaded by the UK IBD Genetics Consortium for analysis.

When logged in, users of the website can view of a history of the forms that they have submitted. They can also continue to enter data into forms that are partially complete.
