UK Ponds on the Map
for the Freshwater Habitats Trust

myAcorn have worked with the Freshwater Habitats Trust to map ponds in the UK. Markers representing ponds are displayed on a selection of Google Maps for different regions of the UK. Ponds are classified into various categories (Priority Ponds, Flagship Ponds etc). Hovering over each marker displays the name of the pond; clicking the marker pops up a box displaying more information, e.g., on accessibility and the data available for that pond.
